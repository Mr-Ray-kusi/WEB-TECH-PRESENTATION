Brief Introduction

Professor Raymond Kusi is a passionate educator and researcher with over 23 years of experience in Information Technology. Currently serving as a Associate Professor at University Cape Coast, he specializes in Software Development. With a strong commitment to fostering innovation and critical thinking, Professor Kusi has taught a wide range of courses, including Data Structures, Machine Learning, Programing and Problem Anaalysis and many more.
